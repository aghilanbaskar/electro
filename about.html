The Electricity Billing and Management System is a comprehensive solution designed to streamline and automate the processes associated with electricity billing. This system efficiently manages customer accounts, meter readings, and billing calculations. With user-friendly interfaces, it provides real-time access to consumption data, enabling accurate and transparent billing. Additionally, the system facilitates payment processing, alerts for overdue payments, and generates detailed reports for better financial analysis. Emphasizing efficiency and reliability, it enhances the overall management of electricity services, benefiting both utility providers and consumers.
